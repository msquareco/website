Frequently Asked Questions

1. Why invest in Dubai instead of other global cities? +

Dubai offers a tax-free environment, high rental yields, strong capital appreciation, and investor-friendly regulations — all within a globally connected, safe, and vibrant economy.

2. How much do I need to get started? +

You can begin investing with as little as AED 500,000 (~USD 135,000), depending on the property type and area. We tailor options to your budget.

3. What’s the buying process for foreign investors? +

The process is streamlined: choose a property, sign a sales agreement, transfer funds securely, and receive your title deed. We guide you at every step.

4. What kind of returns can I expect? +

Typical rental yields range from 6–10%, and capital appreciation averages 5–8% annually, depending on the area and asset class.

5. Do I need to be in Dubai for the process? +

No. Most of our clients invest remotely. We handle everything — virtual viewings, digital paperwork, and secure transactions — without needing your physical presence.

6. What’s the exit strategy or resale process? +

You can sell anytime through the secondary market or via our network. We help with valuation, listing, and finding buyers for a seamless exit.
